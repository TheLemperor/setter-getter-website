Object-Oriented Programming

Welcome to our Viking Hacks 2022 project. This is a website that aims at being a knowledge source for Object Oriented Programming (OOP), unifying all the different languages information into one quick and easy page.

There is one page describing OOP in general, describing what it looks like, what it does, and the pros and cons of it.

The next three pages talk about what OOP looks like in the different languages, going through syntax and any different behaviors that the language might have.

Project by: Edwin Villalpando, Lemuel Sumardy, Ryan Ardalan, Thomas Yeoh, Tyler Osbey
